### I. DEMAND FOR DISCLOSURE (CCPA Section 1798.110)

Pursuant to CCPA Section 1798.110, I demand that Anthropic disclose:

1. Whether any code, documentation, configuration files, README files, markdown documents, or other content from any of the 1,825+ repositories across BlackRoad's 17 GitHub organizations was included in the training data for any Anthropic model, including but not limited to Claude, Claude 2, Claude 3, Claude 3.5, Claude 4, and any successor models.

2. The specific categories and pieces of data collected from BlackRoad repositories.

3. The sources from which BlackRoad data was collected (e.g., direct GitHub scraping, Common Crawl, The Pile, The Stack, or any third-party data provider).

4. The business or commercial purpose for collecting BlackRoad data.

5. The categories of third parties with whom BlackRoad data has been shared.

6. An itemized accounting of all BlackRoad data in Anthropic's possession, including file paths, repository names, organization names, and timestamps of collection.

---

### II. DEMAND FOR DATA RETURN

I demand that Anthropic provide to BlackRoad OS, Inc. complete copies of all data originating from BlackRoad repositories that exists in Anthropic's systems, including but not limited to:

- Raw training data files
- Processed/tokenized versions
- Embeddings or vector representations
- Cached copies
- Evaluation or benchmark datasets
- Any derived or transformed versions

---

### III. DEMAND FOR DELETION (CCPA Section 1798.105)

Pursuant to CCPA Section 1798.105, I demand that Anthropic:

1. **Delete** all BlackRoad data from all systems, including training pipelines, data lakes, evaluation sets, and any cached or indexed copies.

2. **Direct all service providers and contractors** who have received BlackRoad data to delete it.

3. **Provide written certification** of deletion, signed by a corporate officer under penalty of perjury, within **forty-five (45) calendar days** of receipt of this notice, as required by CCPA Section 1798.105(b).

---

### IV. CEASE AND DESIST

BlackRoad OS, Inc. hereby demands that Anthropic immediately and permanently:

1. **Cease** all use of BlackRoad data for model training, fine-tuning, evaluation, or any other purpose.

2. **Cease** all scraping, crawling, or collection of data from BlackRoad repositories.

3. **Cease** generating outputs that reproduce or derive from BlackRoad proprietary code, documentation, or intellectual property.
